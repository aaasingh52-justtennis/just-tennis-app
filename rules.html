1. Introduction

Just Tennis is a free, mixed-gender community tennis league based in Dubai. The season runs from January 1, 2026 to April 1, 2026.

The league is designed to be flexible, friendly, and competitive. There are no fixed match times; players arrange matches themselves.

2. Format & Scoring

Match Format

  • Matches are typically Best of 3 Sets.
  • Players can agree to play a Pro Set (First to 8 or 10) if time is limited.
  • If court time expires, the score at that moment stands (or players can agree to finish later).

Points System

  • 1 point for playing (Participation).
  • 1 point for each set won.
  • 2 points bonus for winning the match.
  • Max Points: 5 points per match.

Example: A 2-1 win gives the winner 5 points (1+2+2) and the loser 2 points (1+1).

3. Scheduling & Locations

Players are responsible for contacting opponents via WhatsApp to arrange matches.

Courts: Matches can be played at any court in Dubai. Court fees are shared 50/50 unless one player offers free access.

Detailed Transfer / Cancellation Policy

If a player cancels a booked court less than 24 hours in advance, they are responsible for the full court fee.

If a player is more than 20 minutes late, the opponent can claim a walkover win (recorded as a 2-0 win).

4. Rankings & Playoffs

Rankings are based on:

  1. Total Points
  2. Match Win Percentage
  3. Set Win Percentage
  4. Registration Order (Earliest joiner wins tie)

Playoffs: The top 16 players at the end of the season qualify for the knockout finals in April.
